**SPX Trend Logic (from PROJECT.md):**
- Bull Market: SPX > 50 SMA > 150 SMA > 200 SMA, all sloping up
- Bull Correction: SPX below 50 SMA but above 150/200, MAs still up
- Transitioning: 50 SMA crossing 150 SMA, mixed slopes
- Bear Market: SPX < 50 SMA < 150 SMA, both sloping down
**Slope calc:** ((recent - older) / older) * 100 over 20 days. > 0.5% = up, < -0.5% = down
